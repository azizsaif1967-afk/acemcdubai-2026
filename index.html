◆ About the Firm

A Dubai consultancy built on trust, rigour and results.

Founded in 2013, Ace Management Consultancy (ACE MC) is a boutique HR and management advisory headquartered opposite Deira City Centre, Dubai. We work at the intersection of people and strategy — helping private groups, family businesses, government-linked entities and fast-growing SMEs design workplaces aligned to the UAE 2040 vision: knowledge-based, Emirati-led, AI-enabled, and globally competitive.

Our philosophy is simple: every business problem is, in the end, a people problem. Every engagement begins by listening — to founders, HR teams, line managers and frontline staff — before we ever recommend a policy, a structure or a system.

  • Independent, senior-led advisory — no junior hand-offs.
  • MOHRE-aligned policies and full UAE labour-law compliance.
  • Global frameworks (SHRM, CIPD) adapted to GCC realities.
  • Arabic & English delivery across the UAE, KSA, Oman, Bahrain.
  • Active BNI Galaxy · North Dubai chapter members — part of Dubai's premier referral network.
“We don't install HR. We transform how work gets done.” — ACE MC Philosophy
